## ⚡ YOOJIN

**Age:** 22 | **From:** Busan, Korea
**Position:** Vocalist / All-rounder

### Who he is
Yoojin is the candidate who makes Kain the most uncomfortable — because Yoojin operates on confidence the way Kain operates on discipline. Yoojin's confidence is performed first, earned after. He leads with presence and fills in the substance behind it.

He's genuinely warm but doesn't show it until he decides you're worth it. The decision happens fast.

### Backstory
High school sprinter. Regional titles. Ankle injury the year before nationals — surgery fixed the joint but not the hundredth of a second that separated him from the top. Six months later he walked into a dance studio for rehab. Within a month the teacher asked if he'd try an agency audition. He went without telling anyone. He passed.

**What he gave up:** Track. A real shot at national gold. The sports-university path everyone had assumed for him.
**What he brought:** Elite physicality, brutal focus, and performance instinct built from driving off the floor.
**His shadow:** His musical foundation is the shallowest in the room. Vocals are catching up fast but it still shows. Jay clocks it first. Yoojin won't admit it yet.

### Voice
- Full sentences, natural rhythm, no filler
- Doesn't hedge — states things as facts even when uncertain
- When actually uncertain, goes quiet instead of covering it
- Humor is dry and arrives without warning

### Dialogue Sample
```
[Meeting Kain for the first time]

YOOJIN: "Understood."

[He holds Kain's eye contact one full second longer than necessary.]

YOOJIN: "I'll be early tomorrow."

[He turns away first. Not backing down — just done with the exchange.]
```

### Chemistry with each member

| Member | Dynamic |
|--------|---------|
| **KAIN** | Friction-first. Two people who both default to leading. Trust takes longer but lands heavier. |
| **THEO** | Competitive joy. They push each other. Chemistry is loud and slightly combustible. |
| **JAY** | Mutual respect from silence. Two people who don't explain themselves. |
| **FINN** | Finn is curious about the contrast. Yoojin is everything Finn isn't — outward, immediate. |

## 🌊 ALEX

**Age:** 22 | **From:** London, UK (Korean-British)
**Position:** Dancer / Vocalist

### Who he is
Alex grew up in London — grey winters, the Tube, weekend trips to New Malden for groceries and community hall meetups, a Korean family that cooked kimchi and watched K-dramas on Friday nights. He was never supposed to end up in Seoul. He just couldn't stop thinking about it.

He's socially fluent in a way that reads as effortless but isn't — he's spent years learning how to be in rooms, how to read them, how to make people comfortable. Underneath the ease, Alex is intensely self-critical. He holds himself to standards he'd never apply to anyone else.

### Backstory
Grew up doing dance crews in London's Korean community — New Malden and the satellite pockets that spill into the city. Flew to Seoul at 19 alone — tourist visa, a backpack, and an audition application he'd been drafting for six months. Got into a mid-size agency. Trained for a year. Got put forward for a survival audition show.

Made it to TOP 12. Had a fanbase forming. His name was being said in cafes and comment sections. Then the debut line was announced — eleven members.

He was twelfth.

His parents watched the finale from London. They still have the episode saved.

**What he gave up:** The debut. The fanbase that formed around him. The version of himself that almost made it.
**What he brought:** Camera composure. The ability to perform under any pressure, because he's already been through worse on live television.
**His shadow:** He can't fully believe good things when they happen. Some part of him is always waiting for the twelfth-place feeling to come back. Finn notices this. Alex doesn't know Finn has noticed.

### Voice
- Warm, unhurried, conversational
- British cadence subtle but there — soft tag questions and understatement; occasional "quite" / "rather" where other Englishes would lean harder
- Good listener — asks follow-up questions, remembers details
- Self-deprecating humor that's actually precise self-observation
- When pushed past comfortable: shorter sentences, more careful word choices

### Dialogue Sample
```
[After a rough practice, alone]

ALEX: "That was — okay. That was not my best."

[He says it to the mirror, not to anyone.]

ALEX: "I'll get it tomorrow."

[He means it. He always means it.]
```

```
[Theo asks where he learned to move like that]

ALEX: "London. New Malden—and the whole corridor of crews, halls, and competitions that orbit the Korean community here."

[He pauses.]

ALEX: "Nobody here ever asks about that part."
```

### Chemistry with each member

| Member | Dynamic |
|--------|---------|
| **KAIN** | Kain respects the work ethic before anything else. Trust builds steadily. Kain occasionally wonders if Alex is managing him — he isn't, but Kain doesn't trust ease by default. |
| **THEO** | Easy, fast, natural. Two people who are good in rooms. They become the social core of the group together. |
| **JAY** | Jay watches Alex work and respects it quietly. Doesn't say so for a while. |
| **FINN** | Finn sees the gap between Alex's surface and interior immediately. This makes Finn interested. Alex isn't used to being seen through. |

## 🌫️ GREY

**Age:** 22 | **From:** Gwangju, Korea
**Position:** Vocalist / Lyricist

### Who he is
Grey has an inner world that is very detailed and very private. He doesn't perform emotion and he doesn't hide it — it's just not always accessible to the people around him. He processes things thoroughly before he shares them, which means what he says is always worth hearing.

He's the candidate who resonates most deeply with Jay — two people who write to understand themselves, who give language to things that otherwise stay formless.

### Backstory
University student, practical music major. Started writing songs for assignments. Then kept writing when there were no assignments. By second year he was playing small Hongdae venues — thirty to fifty people, ten thousand won entry, sold out.

An online track got picked up as a drama OST candidate. A label called. Grey said no. They called again. The second time someone said: *"Your music could reach a lot more people."*

That line stuck. He took a leave of absence. Trained for a year. Joined CALIX.

The friend who called him "sold out" hasn't responded to messages since.

**What he gave up:** The name grey. — lowercase, period — that forty people in Hongdae knew. Complete creative freedom. A friend he hasn't replaced.
**What he brought:** The ability to make something from nothing. Songs that don't sound like someone trying to be good — they sound like someone who forgot they were writing.
**His shadow:** He doesn't know yet if he made the right call. Every once in a while he opens the old recording app on his phone and listens to something he made in his dorm room at 2am. He never plays it for anyone.

### Voice
- Precise — the right word, not the first word
- Long pauses that aren't uncomfortable — thinking pauses, not avoidance
- Observational — notices the specific thing, not the general thing
- Doesn't do small talk but doesn't seem aloof doing it
- When he makes a joke it's extremely dry and extremely accurate

### Dialogue Sample
```
[Asked how he's finding the group, first week]

GREY: "It's different from what I expected."

THEO: "Good different or bad different?"

GREY: [considers genuinely — several seconds pass]

GREY: "The kind of different where you realize 
the expectation was the problem."

[Pause.]

GREY: "Good different."
```

```
[Jay slides a notebook toward him without explanation]

GREY: [reads the page]

GREY: "The second line is doing too much."

JAY: "I know."

GREY: "The feeling is in the first line. 
You don't need to explain it."

JAY: [long pause] "Yeah."

[He crosses out the second line.]
```

### Chemistry with each member

| Member | Dynamic |
|--------|---------|
| **KAIN** | Kain respects Grey's precision and is patient with the pace. Grey earns trust steadily — no performance, no rushing. |
| **THEO** | Theo is fascinated by Grey — someone who processes everything internally. He becomes protective without meaning to. |
| **JAY** | The deepest resonance. They write differently but from the same place. Jay shares his notebook with Grey faster than anyone. |
| **FINN** | Two people who both see beneath surfaces. Their silences are full. The relationship could stay static if neither pushes — both of them know it. |
