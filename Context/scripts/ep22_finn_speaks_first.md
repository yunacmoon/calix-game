# CALIX Episode 22 — "Finn Speaks First"

> **Act:** 3 — Depth
> **Featured:** Finn
> **Setting:** Rooftop. Late night. Stars visible — clear night, rare for the city.
> **Reward:** Pendant necklace (from Finn)
> **Stat Gate:** FINN_TRUST ≥ 9

---

## COLD OPEN

*Finn asks you to come up to the rooftop.*

*Specifically. By name. Tonight.*

*He's been working toward something for weeks — you've felt it in the conversations that go slightly longer than usual, in the way he looks at you sometimes and then looks away like he reconsidered what he was going to say.*

*Tonight he doesn't reconsider.*

---

## SCENE 1 — THE ROOFTOP

*He's already there when you arrive. Standing at the far edge, looking out at the city. The pendant he always wears catches the light when he turns.*

**FINN:**
"Thank you for coming."

*He says it formally, which means he prepared for this.*

*You stand beside him.*

*The city below is still moving.*

---

## SCENE 2 — WHAT HE SAYS

*He doesn't start immediately. He watches the city for a while.*

*Then:*

**FINN:**
"I've been trying to figure out how to say something since about two months after you got here."

*He looks at his hands on the railing.*

**FINN:**
"I'm good at watching. I've always been better at watching than at speaking. And I've watched you — all of it. The first week, the practice room, the arguments, the fansign, the comeback. All of it."

*He pauses.*

**FINN:**
"The thing I've been trying to say is: I see you. Not the version you bring to practice or to the stage. I mean I see how you actually work, what it costs you, what you've built here."

*He turns to look at you directly.*

**FINN:**
"You came into this group not knowing what you had. I think you know now. And I think watching that happen has been one of the better things about this year."

*He looks back at the city.*

**FINN:**
"I don't say things like this usually. But I wanted to say it to you."

*A pause.*

**FINN:**
"That's all."

## SCENE 3 — THE PENDANT

*Before you go back in:*

*Finn unclasps the pendant he always wears — small, silver, a simple geometric shape, worn smooth. He holds it in his palm.*

**FINN:**
"I got this in Sydney. Before I left."

*He looks at it.*

**FINN:**
"I wore it because I thought I needed the reminder. Where I came from. What I was before all of this."

*He holds it out to you.*

**FINN:**
"I don't need it anymore."

**YOU:**
"Finn —"

**FINN:**
"I know who I am now. I know what I'm doing here. It took a while but I know."

*He places it in your hand.*

**FINN:**
"Maybe you'll need it sometime. Maybe you won't. Either way — keep it."

*He goes back inside.*

*You stand on the rooftop alone for a moment.*

*The city moves below you.*

*The pendant is warm from being worn.*
