# CALIX Episode 26 — "Kain Asks"
# ACT 4 — RESOLUTION BEGINS

> **Act:** 4 — Resolution
> **Featured:** Kain
> **Setting:** Rooftop. One chair, one thermos. He was here first.
> **Reward:** 1200 coins
> **Stat Gate:** KAIN_TRUST ≥ 12

---

## COLD OPEN

*You find Kain on the rooftop.*

*He's sitting in the one folding chair someone left up here months ago. He has a thermos. He's looking at the city.*

*He heard you come up. He doesn't turn.*

**KAIN:**
"Sit somewhere."

*There's no other chair. You sit on the ledge — safe distance from the edge. He watches you settle without looking directly at you.*

*He pours from the thermos.*

*He hands it to you first.*

*You've been here a year and this is the first time he does that.*

---

## SCENE 1 — THE QUESTION

*He drinks from the cap of the thermos.*

*The city below is doing its usual thing.*

*He lets the silence run for a while — not the uncomfortable kind. Kain's silences don't ask anything of you. They just exist.*

*Then:*

**KAIN:**
"I want to ask you something."

**YOU:**
"Okay."

**KAIN:**
"Not a performance answer. Not what the label wants to hear. Not what you think I want to hear."

*He looks at the city.*

**KAIN:**
"What do you think CALIX's next chapter looks like."

*He holds the question open.*

**KAIN:**
"We have a renewal coming. A new cycle. I've been building a plan — but I've been building it alone again. And I —"

*He stops.*

*He starts again.*

**KAIN:**
"I want to know what you see. You came in from outside. You see things I've been too close to see."

*He looks at you.*

**KAIN:**
"Tell me."

---

## CHOICE 1 — Kain asks for your real vision for CALIX's next chapter.

---

**OPTION A:** *"The next cycle should be the one where CALIX stops explaining itself."*
> *You think about it. Really think.*
>
> **YOU:** "I think the next cycle should be the one where CALIX stops explaining itself. The first two years were about proving you belonged in the conversation. You're in the conversation now. Start saying something in it."
>
> *Kain is listening.*
>
> **YOU:** "Jay has tracks he hasn't released because they didn't fit the format. Move the format toward Jay, not the other way around. Theo wants floor work on every stage — let him have it. Finn wants to be heard before he's seen — give him a track that does that."
>
> *You look at him.*
>
> **YOU:** "And you need to stop carrying the whole thing alone. You've been CALIX's spine for three years. Let it be a body."
>
> *A long silence.*
>
> *Kain looks at his thermos.*
>
> *He takes out the pocket notebook he carries and writes something.*
>
> **KAIN:** "Keep going."
>
> `[+5 KAIN_TRUST] [+3 GROUP_REP]`

---

**OPTION B:** *"Ask the group. Together."*
> **YOU:** "I think this is the conversation you should be having with all five of us."
>
> *Kain looks at you.*
>
> **YOU:** "Not because I don't have thoughts. But because the thing I'd tell you is: the next chapter is the one where decisions like this happen in the room, not on a rooftop between two people."
>
> *A pause.*
>
> **KAIN:** "..."
>
> *He looks at the city.*
>
> **KAIN:** "That's a fair answer."
>
> *He pours more from the thermos.*
>
> **KAIN:** "I'll set the meeting."
>
> `[+4 KAIN_TRUST] [+4 GROUP_REP]`

---

**OPTION C:** *"What do you actually want?"*
> **YOU:** "What do you want? Not what the plan needs. You, specifically."
>
> *Kain is very still.*
>
> *A long silence.*
>
> *He looks at the city.*
>
> **KAIN:** "..."
>
> *His hand tightens around the thermos cap.*
>
> **KAIN:** "I want to make something that I'm still proud of in ten years. Not just successful. Proud of it."
>
> *He exhales.*
>
> **KAIN:** "I've been so focused on not failing that I've never properly asked myself what winning looks like."
>
> *He looks at you.*
>
> **KAIN:** "That's the answer, I think. That's where it starts."
>
> `[+6 KAIN_TRUST]`

---

## SCENE 2 — AFTER

*The city keeps doing its thing.*

*At some point the thermos runs out. Kain holds it upside down — nothing left.*

**KAIN:**
"I don't do this."

**YOU:**
"What?"

**KAIN:**
"Ask people things. What they think. What they see."

*A pause.*

**KAIN:**
"I've been doing it with you."

*He sets the thermos down.*

*He looks at you — not assessing. Just looking.*

**KAIN:**
"I don't know what that means yet. But I wanted you to know I noticed."

*He stands.*

*He picks up the thermos.*

**KAIN:**
"Come on. It's cold."

*He holds the rooftop door open.*

*He waits.*
