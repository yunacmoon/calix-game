# CALIX Special Episode — "Seongsu" (Jay)

> **Act:** Bonus
> **Featured:** Jay
> **Setting:** A free afternoon. Seongsu-dong. Vintage shops, small brands, a coffee somewhere.
> **Reward:** `300 coins`
> **Stat Gate:** none

---

## SCENE DIRECTION NOTES

*Tone: Jay slightly outside his usual frame — not quite comfortable with shopping, but focused on you. He doesn't want things. He notices what you want.*
*Music: Whatever's playing in the shops. He mentions one song title without explaining why.*
*Camera: His hands touching things without picking them up. The way he watches you decide.*

---

## COLD OPEN

*The text is sparse.*

> **JAY:** Free this afternoon?

*Then, a few minutes later:*

> **JAY:** I need to find a birthday gift. I thought you might have better instincts than me.

*Then, almost immediately:*

> **JAY:** It doesn't have to be about the gift.

*You understand what that means.*

*You go.*

---

## SCENE 1 — THE STREET

*He's there when you arrive. No fanfare. He starts walking when he sees you.*

**JAY:** "Thanks for coming."

**YOU:** "Who's the gift for?"

**JAY:** "Doesn't matter right now."

*He says it like it's true. Like the gift became a reason and the reason is no longer the point.*

**NARRATION:**
> *Seongsu-dong. The converted spaces. Small brands. The afternoon light coming through gaps between buildings.*
>
> *Jay moves through it like he moves through most things — with attention, without urgency. He reads the storefronts the way he reads everything.*

---

## SCENE 2 — THE FIRST SHOP

*He touches things without picking them up. He moves through a rack slowly.*

*He stops at something. Looks at it.*

**JAY:** "What do you think of this?"

*He's asking carefully. Like your answer matters.*

---

## CHOICE 1 — He's asking your opinion. Be honest.

---

**OPTION A:** *"It's good. Understated."*

> **YOU:** "It's good. It doesn't try too hard."
>
> **JAY:** "That's what I thought."
>
> *He takes it off the rack.*
>
> **JAY:** "You have a word for things. You know what something is without having to describe all of it."
>
> **YOU:** "Is that rare?"
>
> **JAY:** "For me, yes."
>
> *He looks at the item.*
>
> **JAY:** "I describe too much. I circle the thing instead of saying it."
>
> **YOU:** "Your writing does that too."
>
> *He looks up.*
>
> **JAY:** "You've noticed that."
>
> **YOU:** "It's good, though. The circling."

---

**OPTION B:** *"The other one. Two racks back."*

> **YOU:** "Go back two racks. There's something better."
>
> *He goes.*
>
> *Finds it.*
>
> *Stands with both.*
>
> **JAY:** "This one."
>
> **YOU:** "That one."
>
> *He puts the first back.*
>
> *He says nothing for a moment.*
>
> **JAY:** "I walked past it three times."
>
> **YOU:** "Sometimes you need someone else to point."
>
> **JAY:** "..."
>
> **JAY:** "Yeah."

---

## SCENE 3 — YOUR TURN

*He's looking at something for himself when he pauses.*

*He turns.*

**JAY:** "What are you looking for?"

**YOU:** "Nothing, I'm just—"

**JAY:** "You've been going through the same section twice."

*He noticed.*

**JAY:** "What is it?"

---

## CHOICE 2 — He picked something for you.

---

**OPTION A:** *[Show him what you were looking at.]*

> *You pull out what you were hesitating over.*
>
> *He looks at it.*
>
> *For a long moment.*
>
> **JAY:** "Get it."
>
> **YOU:** "I don't know if I need—"
>
> **JAY:** "You don't need it. You want it. That's different and it's also fine."
>
> *He says it like it's something he's had to learn himself.*
>
> **JAY:** "Get it."

---

**OPTION B:** *"How did you know I was looking at something?"*

> **YOU:** "How did you notice?"
>
> **JAY:** "I notice you."
>
> *He says it plainly. No emphasis. No drama.*
>
> **JAY:** "I pay attention to what you linger on."
>
> *A beat.*
>
> **JAY:** "You linger on things you want but aren't sure you're allowed to want."
>
> *He takes the item from you. Looks at it.*
>
> **JAY:** "You're allowed."
>
> *He hands it back.*

---

## SCENE 4 — THE COFFEE

*A quieter place. He chooses a table away from the window — old habit.*

*Coffee comes. He turns the cup in his hands.*

**JAY:** "I used to hate shopping."

**YOU:** "Past tense?"

**JAY:** "It's better with someone."

*He looks at the street through the glass.*

**NARRATION:**
> *Jay in public is always slightly braced. Aware of the edges of the space, where the attention is.*
>
> *Here, now, that's loosened.*

---

## CHOICE 3 — Something more honest gets said.

---

**OPTION A:** *"I like who you are when it's just us."*

> **YOU:** "You're different when it's just the two of us."
>
> *He looks at you.*
>
> **JAY:** "Different how."
>
> **YOU:** "Quieter. But more there."
>
> *A long pause.*
>
> **JAY:** "..."
>
> **JAY:** "I think I'm always more there with you."
>
> *He says it to his coffee cup. But he means it.*

---

**OPTION B:** *"You said the gift doesn't matter. What does?"*

> **YOU:** "You said it doesn't have to be about the gift."
>
> **JAY:** "Yeah."
>
> **YOU:** "So what is it about?"
>
> *He thinks.*
>
> **JAY:** "I wanted to see you in a different context."
>
> **YOU:** "Not a practice room."
>
> **JAY:** "Not anywhere that requires something from us."
>
> *A beat.*
>
> **JAY:** "I wanted to see what you're like when nothing's at stake."
>
> **YOU:** "And?"
>
> **JAY:** "Same. You're the same."
>
> *He says it like that's a good thing. Like it's rare.*

---

## SCENE 5 — THE GIFT

*Before you leave the last shop, he buys two things. One for himself. One he wraps in tissue at the counter.*

*He holds it out.*

**YOU:** "That's not for whoever's birthday it is."

**JAY:** "No."

**YOU:** "Jay."

**JAY:** "You pointed to it in the first shop."

*He says it simply.*

**JAY:** "You thought I didn't notice."

*A beat.*

**YOU:** "I thought I was being subtle."

**JAY:** "You weren't."

*He holds it steady.*

**NARRATION:**
> *You take it.*
>
> *He turns to leave.*
>
> *And then, quietly:*

**JAY:** "I notice everything about you."

*He doesn't wait for a response.*

*He just walks, and you follow, and neither of you says anything.*

*That's enough.*

*— End of Special Episode —*
