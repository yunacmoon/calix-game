# CALIX Episode 25 — "After the Stage"

> **Act:** 3 — Depth
> **Featured:** All members
> **Setting:** Backstage. Just the five of you. Door closed.
> **Reward:** 1000 coins
> **Stat Gate:** None (emotional beat)

---

## COLD OPEN

*The stage door closes.*

*The five of you are in the backstage room.*

*Nobody says anything.*

*The crowd noise is still there — muffled now, outside — but you can feel it in your chest.*

*One minute of silence.*

*Just breathing.*

---

## SCENE 1 — THE ROOM

*Theo makes a sound first. Not words — just a sound. Short, involuntary, like something escaping.*

*Then:*

**THEO:**
"Okay."

*He sits down on the floor. Just — sits.*

**THEO:**
"Okay. Yeah."

*Jay says one word.*

**JAY:**
"Good."

*He means the show. He means everything.*

*Kain sits down. Not on the floor — on the equipment case by the wall. He uncaps his water. He drinks. He doesn't say anything.*

*But he sits down, and for Kain, sitting down after a performance without immediately moving to the next thing is the equivalent of anyone else crying.*

*Finn catches your eye.*

*He smiles.*

*It's specific — the kind of smile that means: I told you. Months ago. I told you.*
