# CALIX Episode 25 — "After the Stage"

> **Act:** 3 — Depth
> **Featured:** All members
> **Setting:** Backstage. Just the five of you. Door closed.
> **Reward:** 1000 coins
> **Stat Gate:** None (emotional beat)

---

## COLD OPEN

*The stage door closes.*

*The five of you are in the backstage room.*

*Nobody says anything.*

*The crowd noise is still there — muffled now, outside — but you can feel it in your chest. The low thrum of something that hasn't fully ended yet.*

*One minute of just breathing.*

---

## SCENE 1 — THE ROOM

*Theo makes a sound first. Not words — just a sound. Short, involuntary.*

*Then:*

**THEO:**
"Okay."

*He sits down on the floor.*

**THEO:**
"Okay. Yeah."

*Jay says one word.*

**JAY:**
"Good."

*He means the show. He means everything.*

*Kain sits down on the equipment case by the wall. He uncaps his water. He drinks.*

*He doesn't say anything.*

*He just sits there — and for Kain, sitting down after a performance without immediately moving to the next thing is a significant event.*

*Finn catches your eye.*

*He smiles.*

*The specific kind that means: I told you. Back at the beginning, in the common room, at 2am — I told you.*

## CHOICE — In the quiet after the stage, what do you feel?

---

**OPTION A:** Relief.
> *You feel the weight leave your body — months of it, a year of it. Not gone, just set down.*
>
> *You lean against the wall.*
>

>

> *Kain on the equipment case. Theo on the floor. Jay against the far wall. Finn in the corner.*
>
