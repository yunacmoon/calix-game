> **Reward:** 350 coins

## SCENE DIRECTION NOTES

*Tone: Light, character-driven. The gap between who people are face-to-face versus in text reveals more than it should.*
*Music: None in the traditional sense — scene changes are marked by notification sounds, typing indicators, the rhythm of a conversation that lives on phones.*
*Format: This episode is partly visual novel, partly text message display. When showing the group chat, format shifts to message bubbles. Stage directions describe the physical scenes between.*

## SCENE 2 — THE VOICE MEMO

*Theo's 1:47 voice memo plays.*

*It is Theo, at full Theo, talking through an idea about the third section of choreo — he thinks the current setup has everyone too static in the second half of the section, and he wants more floor work, or at least more dynamic variation, and he has a specific sequence in mind that he's humming to demonstrate and the humming is genuinely good and also he mentions twice that he doesn't want to step on Kain's plan but he's also being very clear about what he thinks —*
