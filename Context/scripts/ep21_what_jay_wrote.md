# CALIX Episode 21 — "What Jay Wrote"

> **Act:** 3 — Depth
> **Featured:** Jay
> **Setting:** Recording studio. Jay calls you in to hear the finished track.
> **Reward:** Silver ring (from Jay)
> **Stat Gate:** JAY_TRUST ≥ 9

---

## COLD OPEN

*The track Jay was working on in EP09 is done.*

*He texts you: studio. now if you can.*

*No explanation. He never explains. But you know the studio room by now — the small one on the third floor with the chair shaped to him and the sticky tabs on the console.*

*He's already there when you arrive. Seated. He doesn't turn around when you come in.*

**JAY:**
"Close the door."

---

## SCENE 1 — THE PLAY

*He doesn't explain what you're about to hear. He presses play.*

*The track fills the room.*

*It's finished now — what you heard in fragments months ago has become something complete. The pulse, the sample, the verses. Jay's voice moving through it like water finding the lowest point.*

*You listen.*

*Second verse. Something shifts in the cadence — a specific line, slowed, opened up. The words are about —*

*They're about staying. About choosing something difficult over something easier. About a specific kind of attention that one person pays to another that changes the weight of everything.*

*It's buried. If you weren't listening closely you'd miss it.*

*You're listening closely.*

*The track ends.*

*Jay is looking at the wall.*

---

## SCENE 2 — THE RING

*Jay is shutting down the console.*

*He does something with his right hand — automatic, like he's done it a hundred times — and then stops.*

*He looks at the ring on his right hand. Silver. He's worn it since before debut. You've seen it in every photo.*

*He takes it off.*

*He looks at you.*

*He holds it out.*

**JAY:**
"Keep it for a while."

*He says it like it's a simple request.*

**YOU:**
"Jay —"

**JAY:**
"Not forever. For a while."

*He sets it in your hand.*

**JAY:**
"I wrote the song. That's enough. This is — something else."

*He picks up his notebook. Stands.*

**JAY:**
"Don't read into it."

*He walks out.*

*You look at the ring in your hand.*

**NARRATION:**
> *Don't read into it.*
>
> *You put it on.*
>
> *You read into it.*
