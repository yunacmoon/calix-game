# CALIX Episode 21 — "What Jay Wrote"

> **Act:** 3 — Depth
> **Featured:** Jay
> **Setting:** Recording studio. Jay calls you in to hear the finished track.
> **Reward:** Silver ring (from Jay)
> **Stat Gate:** JAY_TRUST ≥ 9

---

## COLD OPEN

*The track Jay was working on in EP09 is done.*

*He texts you: studio. now if you can.*

*No explanation. He never explains. But you know the studio room by now — the small one on the third floor with the chair shaped to him and the sticky tabs on the console.*

*He's already there when you arrive. Seated. He doesn't turn around when you come in.*

**JAY:**
"Close the door."

---

## SCENE 1 — THE PLAY

*He doesn't say anything. He presses play.*

*The track fills the room.*

*It's finished now — what you heard in fragments months ago has become something complete. The pulse, the sample, the verses. Jay's voice moving through it like it has somewhere specific to go.*

*You listen.*

*Second verse. Something shifts in the cadence — a specific line, slowed, opened up. About staying. About choosing the hard thing. About the particular weight of someone being present when you're working something out and not making you explain it.*

*It's buried. You'd miss it if you weren't paying close attention.*

*You're paying close attention.*

*The track ends.*

*Jay is looking at the wall.*

---

## CHOICE 1 — When it ends. The silence.

---

**OPTION A:** Say the specific line. Out loud.
> *You hold it for a beat.*
>
> *Then you say the line. The one from the second verse. Quietly, just the words back.*
>
> *Jay goes very still.*
>
> *A long silence.*
>
> **JAY:** "..."
>
> *He exhales. Slow.*
>
> **JAY:** "Yeah."
>
> *He doesn't explain it. He doesn't qualify it.*
>
> **JAY:** "I just needed you to hear it finished. That's all."
>
> `[+6 JAY_TRUST]`

---

**OPTION B:** *"It's the best thing you've made."*
> **YOU:** "It's the best thing you've made."
>
> *Jay is quiet.*
>
> **JAY:** "..."
>
> *He turns slightly — not all the way. Profile.*
>
> **JAY:** "You heard it."
>
> *He doesn't say which part he means. He can tell from the way you said it.*
>
> **JAY:** "Okay."
>
> *He saves the file.*
>
> `[+5 JAY_TRUST]`

---

**OPTION C:** Ask if the others know yet.
> **YOU:** "Have the others heard this?"
>
> *Jay shakes his head.*
>
> **JAY:** "No."
>
> **YOU:** "Why me first?"
>
> *He looks at the console.*
>
> **JAY:** "Because you were in the room when I was making it. It made sense to finish the loop."
>
> *He says it plainly. Like a technical answer. It is, for him.*
>
> `[+3 JAY_TRUST]`

---

## SCENE 2 — THE RING

*Jay is shutting down the console.*

*He does something with his right hand — automatic, like he's done it a hundred times — and then stops.*

*He looks at the ring on his right hand. Silver. He's worn it since before debut. You've seen it in every photo.*

*He takes it off.*

*He looks at you.*

*He holds it out.*

**JAY:**
"Keep it for a while."

*He says it like it's a simple request.*

**YOU:**
"Jay —"

**JAY:**
"Not forever. For a while."

*He sets it in your hand.*

**JAY:**
"I wrote the track. That's enough. This is just — something else."

*He picks up his notebook. Stands.*

**JAY:**
"Don't make it weird."

*He walks out.*

*You look at the ring in your hand.*

**NARRATION:**
> *The track is finished. He made something out of a year of paying attention, and now it exists.*
>
> *You put the ring on.*
>
> *The studio is quiet.*
