# CALIX Special Episode — "Seongsu" (Kain)

> **Act:** Bonus
> **Featured:** Kain
> **Setting:** A free afternoon. Seongsu-dong. Vintage shops, small brands, a coffee somewhere.
> **Reward:** `300 coins`
> **Stat Gate:** none

---

## SCENE DIRECTION NOTES

*Tone: Kain completely out of his domain — shopping, aesthetics, choosing things for someone else. He does it exactly as you'd expect: precise, unhurried, quietly certain.*
*Music: Whatever's playing in the shops. He doesn't comment on it.*
*Camera: His hands going through a rack. His expression when something catches his eye.*

---

## COLD OPEN

*It's his idea. That's what surprises you.*

*Kain texts in the afternoon:*

> **KAIN:** Seongsu. An hour.

*Then:*

> **KAIN:** There are two shops I want to look at. You can come.

*It's not exactly an invitation. But you know what it is.*

*You go.*

---

## SCENE 1 — THE STREET

*Seongsu-dong on a weekday afternoon. The converted warehouses and small storefronts. Young people. The smell of coffee from somewhere.*

*He walks like he always does — deliberate, unhurried, aware of everything around him without looking like he is. A mask and a cap. Nobody stops him.*

*He stops in front of a small vintage shop.*

**KAIN:** "This one."

*He goes in without waiting.*

**NARRATION:**
> *The shop is narrow and well-curated. Someone put thought into what goes here.*
>
> *Kain moves through it like he's reading it. A hand on one rack, a pause, a move forward.*

---

## SCENE 2 — THE FIRST SHOP

*He pulls out a jacket early. Holds it up. Puts it back.*

*He pulls out a second one. Holds it for longer.*

**KAIN:** "What do you think of this?"

*He's asking genuinely. He holds it out toward you.*

**YOU:** "For you?"

**KAIN:** "Obviously."

*You look at it.*

---

## SCENE 3 — YOUR TURN

*He moves through two more racks methodically. Then he stops and looks at you.*

**KAIN:** "Your turn."

**YOU:** "I'm just looking."

**KAIN:** "No. Find something."

*He says it like an assignment. He means it.*

*He watches you go through the rack.*

**NARRATION:**
> *There's something strange about being watched by Kain while you choose. Not uncomfortable — but precise. Like he's cataloguing something.*

*You pull something out. Hold it up.*

*He looks at it for a moment.*

**KAIN:** "No."

**YOU:** "No?"

**KAIN:** "Wrong proportions for you."

*He goes past you down the rack. Stops. Pulls something out.*

**KAIN:** "Try this."

---

## CHOICE 2 — He picked something for you.

---

**OPTION A:** *[Try it on.]*

> *You try it on.*
>
> *He looks at it.*
>
> *For a moment he says nothing. Just looks.*
>
> **KAIN:** "Yeah."
>
> *That's all.*
>
> *But the way he says it.*
>
> **YOU:** "That's all I get?"
>
> **KAIN:** "It's right. What else is there to say."
>
> *He turns back to the rack. But he waits for you to take it off the hanger.*

---

**OPTION B:** *"How did you know that would work?"*

> **YOU:** "How did you know that would be right?"
>
> **KAIN:** "I pay attention."
>
> **YOU:** "To my proportions?"
>
> **KAIN:** "..."
>
> *He looks at you steadily.*
>
> **KAIN:** "To you."
>
> *He says it like it's obvious. Like it's always been obvious.*
>
> **KAIN:** "Keep it."

---

## SCENE 4 — THE COFFEE

*A small place nearby. He orders without ceremony. You sit outside, cups between you, the street moving around you.*

*He's quiet. That's normal.*

*But there's something different about this quiet. Less guarded.*

**NARRATION:**
> *Kain in a practice room is a set of responsibilities. Kain on a stage is a performance.*
>
> *Kain on a street in Seongsu, coffee in hand, looking at people go by — that's something else.*

**KAIN:** "You always come when I ask."

*It's not a question. But it's not nothing.*

**YOU:** "You don't ask often."

**KAIN:** "No."

*A beat.*

---

## CHOICE 3 — Something more honest gets said.

---

**OPTION A:** *"I like that you ask me."*

> **YOU:** "I like it when you do."
>
> *He looks at you.*
>
> **KAIN:** "..."
>
> *He looks back at the street.*
>
> **KAIN:** "Good."
>
> *A long pause.*
>
> **KAIN:** "I'll ask more."
>
> *Quiet. Direct. He means it.*

---

**OPTION B:** *"Why me?"*

> **YOU:** "Why do you ask me specifically?"
>
> *He drinks his coffee.*
>
> **KAIN:** "You don't require anything from me."
>
> **YOU:** "Meaning?"
>
> **KAIN:** "Most people want something when they spend time with me. A version of me. A performance."
>
> *He sets the cup down.*
>
> **KAIN:** "You just want to be in the same place."
>
> *A pause.*
>
> **KAIN:** "That's rarer than you think."

---

## SCENE 5 — THE GIFT

*On the way back to the street, he stops in front of a small shop. He looks in the window for a moment.*

*He goes in. He's inside for less than three minutes.*

*He comes out with a small bag. He holds it out.*

**KAIN:** "For the thing you almost bought in the first shop."

*You look at him.*

**YOU:** "I put it back."

**KAIN:** "I know."

*He holds the bag steady.*

**KAIN:** "Take it."

*He says it like the conversation is over. It is.*

**NARRATION:**
> *You don't say thank you. You know he doesn't want you to.*
>
> *You take the bag.*
>
> *He nods once. Starts walking.*
>
> *You follow.*
>
> *That's enough. For Kain, that's everything.*
