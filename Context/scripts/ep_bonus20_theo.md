# CALIX Special Episode — "Seongsu" (Theo)

> **Act:** Bonus
> **Featured:** Theo
> **Setting:** A free afternoon. Seongsu-dong. Vintage shops, small brands, a coffee somewhere.
> **Reward:** `300 coins`
> **Stat Gate:** none

---

## SCENE DIRECTION NOTES

*Tone: Theo at maximum Theo — excited about everything, wants your opinion on everything, ends up saying something unexpectedly real.*
*Music: He's playing music from his phone out loud and does not care.*
*Camera: His face when he finds something he loves. The way he holds things up.*

---

## COLD OPEN

*The texts come in fast.*

> **THEO:** okay okay okay
> **THEO:** free afternoon, seongsu, YES OR YES
> **THEO:** i found three shops i've been wanting to go to and i need a second opinion
> **THEO:** please don't make me go alone i get too excited and buy everything

*You're already laughing.*

*You go.*

---

## SCENE 1 — THE STREET

*He's waiting outside when you arrive. He's wearing something casual but somehow it looks intentional. He clocks your outfit immediately.*

**THEO:** "Okay that's cute, we have to find something to go with that."

**YOU:** "I'm just here to advise."

**THEO:** "Sure you are."

*He's already moving toward the first shop.*

**NARRATION:**
> *Seongsu-dong on a weekday. Young people. Coffee. Converted spaces with curated interiors.*
>
> *Theo in Seongsu-dong is immediately in his environment. He clocks every window display. He comments on all of them.*

---

## SCENE 2 — THE FIRST SHOP

*He goes through the rack with speed and certainty. Items come off, get assessed, go back. He has opinions about everything.*

**THEO:** "Okay what about this—"

*He holds up something large. Dramatic. Very him.*

**YOU:** "That's very you."

**THEO:** "That's what I thought."

*He holds it against himself.*

**THEO:** "Too much?"

## SCENE 3 — YOUR TURN

*Three minutes later, he's found something for you.*

*He didn't ask. He just appears with it.*

**THEO:** "OKAY. This. For you. Right now. Try it."

*He holds it out with genuine excitement.*

**NARRATION:**
> *Theo picks things for people the way he does everything — with complete conviction and zero hesitation.*
>
> *He's also, you've noticed, usually right.*

---

## SCENE 4 — THE COFFEE

*A place with outdoor seating. He orders something with too many modifiers and receives it without judgment. You sit across from each other.*

*He's mid-sentence about the third shop when he stops.*

**THEO:** "Can I say something?"

**YOU:** "You've been saying things."

**THEO:** "Something real."

*You look at him.*

**NARRATION:**
> *Theo talks constantly. But there's a difference between Theo filling space and Theo choosing to say something.*

**THEO:** "I'm really happy today."

*A pause.*

**THEO:** "I don't always say that when it's true. I'm trying to say it more."

---

## SCENE 5 — THE GIFT

*On the way out of the last shop, he spots something in a small display by the register. He picks it up, looks at it, puts it down, picks it up again.*

*He buys it without telling you.*

*Outside, he hands it to you. A small thing — something from the shop.*

**THEO:** "Because you said you liked it when we walked past."

**YOU:** "I didn't say that."

**THEO:** "You made a face."

**YOU:** "What face?"

**THEO:** "The face you make when you want something but you're deciding if it's reasonable."

*He holds it out.*

**THEO:** "It's reasonable."

**NARRATION:**
> *He's right. You made the face.*
>
> *You take it.*
>
> *He looks genuinely pleased.*
>
> *That's the thing about Theo — giving things makes him happier than getting them.*
