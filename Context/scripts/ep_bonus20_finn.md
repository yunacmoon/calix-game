# CALIX Special Episode — "Seongsu" (Finn)

> **Act:** Bonus
> **Featured:** Finn
> **Setting:** A free afternoon. Seongsu-dong. Vintage shops, small brands, a coffee somewhere.
> **Reward:** `300 coins`
> **Stat Gate:** none

---

## SCENE DIRECTION NOTES

*Tone: Finn quietly delighted — he photographs this neighborhood, and now he's in it with you. He says less than the moment deserves but does more.*
*Music: He has earbuds in one ear. The other is open.*
*Camera: Obviously. He brought the camera.*

---

## COLD OPEN

*The text arrives mid-morning.*

> **FINN:** Are you doing anything this afternoon?

*Then:*

> **FINN:** I'm going to Seongsu. There's a shop I want to go into. And some walls I want to photograph.

*Then, after a pause long enough that you thought that was the whole message:*

> **FINN:** You could come if you want. I'd like that.

*That last part. The way it lands.*

*You go.*

---

## SCENE 1 — THE STREET

*He's waiting when you arrive. The camera is around his neck — of course it is. He looks at you when you approach, and something in his expression settles. Like he'd been slightly uncertain you'd actually come.*

**FINN:** "Hey."

**YOU:** "Hey."

*He starts walking. You fall in beside him.*

**NARRATION:**
> *Seongsu-dong on a weekday afternoon. Converted warehouses. Young people. Murals on side streets.*
>
> *Finn walks slowly. He's looking at everything — the light, the textures, the negative space between buildings.*
>
> *He stops twice before you even reach the first shop. Takes two photos of things you might have walked past without seeing.*

**FINN:** "Sorry. I get distracted."

**YOU:** "Don't be sorry."

---

## SCENE 2 — THE FIRST SHOP

*Small. Curated. The person running it has clearly thought about every item in the space.*

*Finn moves through it quietly. He picks things up with care and puts them down the same way.*

*He stops at a jacket. Holds it up. Looks at it.*

*He holds it out toward you.*

**FINN:** "What do you think?"

*He genuinely wants to know.*

---

## SCENE 3 — YOUR TURN

*He's looking at something else when he goes still.*

*He looks at you.*

**FINN:** "Wait."

*He goes two racks over. He looks. He comes back with something.*

*He holds it out without explanation.*

**NARRATION:**
> *He does this sometimes — arrives at a conclusion you haven't reached yet and offers it to you before you can find your way there.*

---

## SCENE 4 — THE COFFEE

*He finds a place with good light. He always finds places with good light.*

*He sits across from you and takes his camera off. Sets it on the table. Like he's putting it down on purpose.*

**NARRATION:**
> *Finn without his camera is Finn without a layer of translation between himself and the moment.*
>
> *He's looking at you directly.*

**FINN:** "I don't always know what to say."

**YOU:** "You say the right things."

**FINN:** "I say the true things. That's not always the same."

---

## CHOICE 3 — Something more honest gets said.

---

**OPTION A:** *"I like that you say true things."*

> **YOU:** "I'd rather have true than right."
>
> **FINN:** "..."
>
> *He looks at you for a moment.*
>
> **FINN:** "Me too."
>
> *A long pause.*
>
> **FINN:** "Then — I'm glad you came today. That's the true thing."
>
> *He says it to his cup. But he means it for you.*

---

**OPTION B:** *"What's the true thing you want to say right now?"*

> **YOU:** "What's the true thing right now?"
>
> *He's quiet.*
>
> **FINN:** "..."
>
> **FINN:** "That I take a lot of photos so I can hold onto things."
>
> *A beat.*
>
> **FINN:** "And that lately I keep wanting to hold onto things that involve you."
>
> *He doesn't look away.*
>
> **FINN:** "That's the true thing."

---

## SCENE 5 — THE GIFT

*Outside the last shop, he stops.*

*He goes back in alone. He's inside for a few minutes.*

*He comes out with a small bag.*

*He doesn't explain it. He holds it out.*

**YOU:** "Finn."

**FINN:** "There was a print inside. Limited run. Street photography — someone's series on Seoul markets."

*He holds the bag steady.*

**FINN:** "You looked at it for a long time when we walked past."

*A beat.*

**YOU:** "I didn't say anything."

**FINN:** "You didn't have to."

*He looks at you.*

**FINN:** "I pay attention."

**NARRATION:**
> *You take it.*
>
> *He looks at you holding it.*
>
> *He lifts the camera — just once, quickly.*
>
> *The shutter sound.*
>
> *He keeps walking.*
>
> *You follow.*
>
> *Neither of you talks about it.*
>
> *But there's a photo now. Of you, holding something he gave you, on a street in Seongsu.*
>
> *He'll print that one too.*
