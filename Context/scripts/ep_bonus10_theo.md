# CALIX Special Episode — "The Long Drive" (Theo)

> **Act:** Bonus
> **Featured:** Theo
> **Setting:** A free day. Theo's car. The road to Incheon. A seaside restaurant. The drive back.
> **Reward:** `200 coins`
> **Stat Gate:** none

---

## SCENE DIRECTION NOTES

*Tone: Theo at full volume, then unexpectedly quiet. The person he is when the performance drops.*
*Music: His playlist, loud at first, then he turns it down when it gets real.*
*Camera: The passenger window. His hands on the wheel. The way he talks with his whole body even while driving.*

---

## COLD OPEN

*You don't expect the text.*

*8:14 AM. A day off — the first real one in three weeks. You were still half asleep.*

*The messages come in rapid succession.*

> **THEO:** HEY
> **THEO:** are you awake
> **THEO:** okay i know it's early but
> **THEO:** do you want to go to the beach

*You stare at the screen.*

> **THEO:** i have a car and a playlist and i really really want raw fish
> **THEO:** please say yes i've been thinking about this since tuesday

*You get dressed.*

---

## SCENE 1 — THE CAR

*His car is exactly what you expected. A hoodie in the back seat. A phone mount he's clearly had arguments with. A playlist already queued up.*

*He's in a great mood. Immediately.*

**THEO:** "OKAY so I mapped three different routes and the fastest one is boring but the scenic one adds like forty minutes but I think it's worth it—"

**YOU:** "Scenic."

**THEO:** "RIGHT? Okay. Yes. Scenic."

*He pulls out of the lot already talking.*

**NARRATION:**
> *The highway opens up here. Seoul falls behind — towers first, then density, then finally sky.*
>
> *Theo sings along to three songs before he realizes he's doing it. He stops. Then keeps going anyway, quieter.*

---

## CHOICE 1 — An hour in the car. What do you talk about?

---

**OPTION A:** *"Do you do this often? Just — drive?"*

> **YOU:** "Do you do this a lot? Just drive somewhere random?"
>
> **THEO:** "Define random."
>
> **YOU:** "Without a real reason."
>
> **THEO:** "Okay no, I always have a reason."
>
> *He taps the steering wheel.*
>
> **THEO:** "The reason is usually that I need to feel like I'm going somewhere. Even if I come back."
>
> **YOU:** "That's kind of beautiful."
>
> **THEO:** "Thank you, I thought of it right now."
>
> *He grins. Then gets slightly more serious.*
>
> **THEO:** "I always invite someone though. I can't do long drives alone. I get too in my head."
>
> **YOU:** "And you invited me."
>
> **THEO:** "..."
>
> *He glances over.*
>
> **THEO:** "You're good at being in the car with me. You just — exist. I like it."

---

**OPTION B:** *"You didn't say where we're going."*

> **YOU:** "Your texts did not include a destination."
>
> **THEO:** "The sea!"
>
> **YOU:** "Which sea."
>
> **THEO:** "The— the west one. The grey one. Incheon area. There's this restaurant—"
>
> *He gestures with one hand, briefly, in a way that explains nothing.*
>
> **YOU:** "You've been there before?"
>
> **THEO:** "My dad used to take us. When I was little."
>
> *A small pause. A different gear.*
>
> **THEO:** "The raw fish is really fresh. Like. Actually fresh. And they give you so much."
>
> **YOU:** "Is that why we're going?"
>
> *He thinks about it.*
>
> **THEO:** "Partly."
>
> *He turns the music down a notch. Just a notch.*
>
> **THEO:** "Mostly I just wanted to show you something I liked."

---

## SCENE 2 — THE SEA

*Incheon. The industrial port gives way to a quieter stretch — smaller fishing boats, the smell of salt. He parks and immediately gets out and stands at the railing above the water and spreads his arms wide.*

**THEO:** "LOOK AT THAT."

**YOU:** "It's grey."

**THEO:** "It's BEAUTIFUL. The grey is part of it."

*He spins around to look at you.*

**THEO:** "You're not moved?"

**YOU:** "I'm moved."

**THEO:** "You don't look moved."

**YOU:** "I'm moved on the inside."

*He laughs — loud, real — and turns back to the water.*

**NARRATION:**
> *There's something about Theo at the water. The noise in him finds somewhere to go.*
>
> *He's still for almost a full minute. That's longer than usual.*

**THEO:** "Okay. raw fish time."

---

## SCENE 3 — THE RESTAURANT

*The restaurant is small. Eight tables. A tank of fish near the entrance. The owner comes out and Theo immediately compliments something about the setup and the owner looks pleased.*

*The raw fish comes out on a wide platter — white flesh, bright against the ice, sesame leaves, fermented soybean paste.*

*Theo eats immediately. And with enthusiasm.*

**THEO:** "Okay. Okay. Wrap it in the leaf with the paste. Like this."

*He demonstrates, then eats, then closes his eyes for a second.*

**THEO:** "That's it. That's the whole reason we drove two hours."

**YOU:** "Was it worth it?"

**THEO:** "Are you kidding me right now."

---

## CHOICE 2 — Eating together. Something gets said.

---

**OPTION A:** *"What made you want to bring someone today?"*

> **YOU:** "Why me today? You could have come alone."
>
> **THEO:** "I told you, I can't do long drives alone—"
>
> **YOU:** "Okay but specifically me."
>
> *He chews. Thinks. Sets down his chopsticks.*
>
> **THEO:** "Because you listen."
>
> **YOU:** "Everyone listens to you."
>
> **THEO:** "No, they hear me. That's different."
>
> *He picks the chopsticks back up.*
>
> **THEO:** "When I talk to you I don't feel like I have to be entertaining. I can just — say things. And they land."
>
> *A beat.*
>
> **THEO:** "That's rare. I don't think you know how rare that is."

---

**OPTION B:** *"Is there something on your mind?"*

> **YOU:** "You've been a little quieter than usual. Even for you."
>
> **THEO:** "I'm never quiet."
>
> **YOU:** "You've been quiet inside the talking."
>
> *He stops.*
>
> **THEO:** "...that is extremely accurate."
>
> *A long pause. He eats another piece.*
>
> **THEO:** "I've been thinking about whether I'm — enough. For this. For the group."
>
> **YOU:** "Theo—"
>
> **THEO:** "Not in a bad way. Just. Honestly. Like, am I pulling my weight in a way that lasts."
>
> *He looks at you.*
>
> **THEO:** "You don't have to say I am."
>
> **YOU:** "I know I don't."
>
> *A beat.*
>
> **YOU:** "You are. And I mean that in a way that lasts."
>
> *He looks at you for a moment.*
>
> **THEO:** "Okay."
>
> *He nods, like he's accepting it carefully.*
>
> **THEO:** "Okay. Thank you."

---

## SCENE 4 — AFTER

*You eat for a long time. He talks about the songs on his playlist, a memory from when his dad brought him here as a kid, something funny that happened at the last rehearsal. You listen to all of it.*

*Outside, after, the light has changed. Afternoon. Pale gold.*

*He leans on the railing and looks at the water.*

**NARRATION:**
> *Theo is usually performing some version of himself. Slightly elevated. Slightly brighter.*
>
> *Here, now, he's just the person underneath.*

*He looks over at you.*

---

## CHOICE 3 — Before the drive back.

---

**OPTION A:** *"I'm glad you texted me."*

> **YOU:** "I'm really glad you texted me this morning."
>
> **THEO:** "Yeah?"
>
> **YOU:** "Yeah."
>
> *He smiles. A real one — the kind he doesn't produce for cameras.*
>
> **THEO:** "Me too."
>
> *He pushes off the railing.*
>
> **THEO:** "Okay. Let's go before I get sad about leaving."

---

**OPTION B:** *"We should do this again."*

> **YOU:** "We should definitely do this again."
>
> **THEO:** "OBVIOUSLY."
>
> *Full volume back.*
>
> **THEO:** "I'm thinking we try the east coast next time. Gangneung. Better fish arguably."
>
> **YOU:** "You've already planned it."
>
> **THEO:** "I planned it in the car on the way here. What can I say."
>
> *He heads toward the car, already talking about the playlist for the drive back.*

---

## DRIVE BACK

*The return is slower — more traffic, the city pulling everything in. He sings along to half the playlist, then goes quiet around the Han bridge.*

*He glances over.*

**THEO:** "Hey."

**YOU:** "Mm."

**THEO:** "Thanks for today. Like — really."

*You look at him.*

**THEO:** "I needed it more than I let on this morning."

**NARRATION:**
> *At some point on the highway, without thinking about it, you fall asleep.*
>
> *When you wake up, you're already back. He's parked. The engine is off.*
>
> *He's watching you with an expression he quickly rearranges.*

**THEO:** "Morning."

**YOU:** "We're back."

**THEO:** "We're back."

*He opens his door.*

*Neither of you mentions the day again. But it happened. And you both know that.*
