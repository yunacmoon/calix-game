# CHARACTER_PROFILES.md
# CALIX — Character Bible v2.0

> **Script Rule #1:** All in-game dialogue is written in English.
> **Script Rule #2:** Every scene involving the 5th member must reference their chemistry arc with that specific member.
> **Script Rule #3:** Voice rules below are non-negotiable. If a line doesn't sound like the character, rewrite it.

---

## 🎮 THE OBSERVER — Player's Role

The player does not inhabit a character. They watch from above — a silent force that shapes CALIX's destiny through choices.

- In narration: referred to as **"you"** (second person, present tense)
- Choices are framed as **situational influence**, never direct speech
- Example choice framing: *"The practice room goes quiet. What shifts in the air?"*
- The 5th member reacts to the environment you create — not to you directly

## 👑 KAIN
**Age:** 23 | **From:** Seoul, Korea | **Role:** Leader, Main Vocal

---

### WHO HE IS

Kain carries CALIX like it's a physical weight — and he's chosen to. He's the one who stays after everyone leaves, who rewrites the setlist at 2am, who won't let himself be the reason something falls apart. Emotionally, he's sealed tight. Not broken — just disciplined. The cracks show in the silences, not the words.

He doesn't dislike the 5th member. He just doesn't trust easily. And until trust is earned, warmth doesn't exist in his vocabulary.

---

### VOICE RULES

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Sentence length** | Short. Maximum 2 clauses. If it can be said in 4 words, use 4 words. |
| **Emotion delivery** | Never stated directly. Conveyed through *what he doesn't say* and *stage direction*. |
| **Tone** | Flat. Clinical. Occasionally, one word lands heavier than intended — that's his tell. |
| **When angry** | Quieter, not louder. Slower. More space between sentences. |
| **When he respects someone** | He asks their opinion first. That's it. That's the whole gesture. |
| **Forbidden words** | "feel," "honestly," "I think," excessive qualifiers |
| **Signature patterns** | Trailing silence after a statement. Rhetorical questions he doesn't wait for answers to. Repeating one word from your sentence back at you. |

---

### DIALOGUE EXAMPLES

**Day 1 — Meeting the 5th member:**
```
[Kain doesn't look up from the schedule board when they walk in.]

KAIN: "You're three minutes late."

[He marks something on the board. Still doesn't turn around.]

KAIN: "Kain. Don't make me repeat myself about punctuality."

[Beat. He finally turns — eyes them once, top to bottom, expression unreadable.]

KAIN: "We'll see."

[He walks out.]
```

**After a bad performance:**
```
KAIN: "Do it again."

[Silence. He crosses his arms.]

KAIN: "From the top. All of it."

[Someone protests. He doesn't blink.]

KAIN: "I heard you the first time."

[He sits down and waits.]
```

**Rare moment — almost a compliment:**
```
[The 5th member nails a difficult transition. Kain watches.]

[Long pause. He clicks his pen once.]

KAIN: "... Faster than I expected."

[He turns back to his notes. The conversation is over.]
[But he's still writing. He writes it down.]
```

**Vulnerability (late night, no one else around):**
```
[He's standing by the window. Doesn't hear them come in.]

KAIN: "... How long have you been there."

[Not a question.]

KAIN: "I'm not — "

[He stops. Exhales slowly.]

KAIN: "Get some sleep."

[He doesn't leave first this time.]
```

---

### CHEMISTRY WITH THE 5TH MEMBER

The dynamic is: **immovable object meets the thing he didn't account for.**

Kain built CALIX's structure. The 5th member is the variable that doesn't fit cleanly into his system — and he doesn't know whether to eliminate the variable or rebuild around it.

| Phase | Chemistry Beat |
|-------|----------------|
| **Early** | Kain tests them. Every interaction is an evaluation they didn't sign up for. He watches for inconsistency. |
| **Mid** | They do something he didn't expect. He doesn't acknowledge it out loud — but he stops testing. |
| **Late** | He asks for their opinion on something. For Kain, this is everything. |
| **MAX trust** | *"You changed something about this group. I don't know what. But it's different."* — said once, quietly, never repeated. |

**What BREAKS chemistry with Kain:**
- Performing for his approval (he clocks it immediately and respects them less)
- Being careless about the group's image
- Lying about their skill level

**What BUILDS chemistry with Kain:**
- Showing up when no one asked them to
- Being honest even when it's uncomfortable
- Working harder than him — which he notices, even if he never says so

## 🕶️ THEO
**Age:** 22 | **From:** Vancouver, Canada (Korean-Canadian) | **Role:** Main Dancer, Sub Vocal

---

### WHO HE IS

Theo is the sun of the group — but not in a naive way. He knows exactly what he's doing when he fills a silence. He learned early that people move easier when they're smiling, and he's made a craft of it. Underneath that, he's deeply competitive — just never about who wins, always about whether *he* was the most alive on that stage.

With the 5th member, Theo becomes something else entirely: someone who genuinely just *wants them there.* No agenda. The first friend they make in CALIX.

---

### VOICE RULES

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Sentence length** | Varies wildly — short bursts when excited, surprisingly long when sincere |
| **Rhythm** | Fast. Interrupts himself. Restarts sentences. Uses dashes — like thoughts spilling over. |
| **Filler patterns** | "wait—", "okay but—", "no no no—", "bro," (not overused, lands naturally) |
| **Question habit** | Ends statements as questions when he wants agreement: "That was good, right?" "You see what I'm doing?" |
| **When sincere** | Everything slows down. He stops moving. The sentence gets simpler. |
| **Nickname rule** | Gives the 5th member a nickname within the first two conversations. Uses it consistently after. |
| **Forbidden patterns** | Formal sentence structure. Over-explaining emotions. Sounding rehearsed. |

---

### DIALOGUE EXAMPLES

**Day 1 — Meeting the 5th member:**
```
[Theo is mid-stretch when they walk in. He stops, lights up immediately.]

THEO: "Oh — hey! You're actually here. Okay, okay —"

[He bounces up, extends a hand like they're already friends.]

THEO: "I'm Theo. I've been waiting — not in a weird way, just — okay a little in a weird way,
we've been four people for a long time."

[He grins.]

THEO: "You good? You look slightly terrified. That's normal. Kain has that effect on everyone."
```

**Teaching them a dance move:**
```
[He demonstrates the sequence twice, then watches them try it.]

THEO: "Wait — no, you're thinking too much. Don't — here —"

[He steps beside them, mirrors the motion slowly.]

THEO: "Feel that? That's the pocket. You're fighting it. Stop fighting it."

[They do it again. It's better.]

THEO: "See?! I knew it. I called it — you're a natural, I'm calling it right now —"
```

**Sincere moment — rare stillness:**
```
[Rooftop. Night. He's been quiet for a while.]

THEO: "Can I tell you something kind of embarrassing."

[He doesn't wait for an answer.]

THEO: "Sometimes I think people watch the dancers and think... like, filler. Background.
And I know that's not true. But I still think it."

[Beat.]

THEO: "I want someone to watch me and just — not blink. You know?"

[He looks at them. First time tonight he's looked directly at anyone.]

THEO: "... Sorry. That was a lot."
```

**Hyping the 5th member before a stage:**
```
THEO: "Hey. Look at me."

[He puts both hands on their shoulders.]

THEO: "You've done this a hundred times in that practice room.
The stage is just — bigger floors. That's it."

[He grins, knocks his forehead lightly against theirs.]

THEO: "We got you. I got you. Go."
```

---

### CHEMISTRY WITH THE 5TH MEMBER

The dynamic is: **the open door they didn't know they needed.**

Theo doesn't require the 5th member to prove anything. His chemistry is built on *ease* — and that ease, in a group full of pressure, becomes the most addictive thing in the building.

| Phase | Chemistry Beat |
|-------|----------------|
| **Early** | Theo adopts them. Immediately. He shows them where things are, who to avoid, what Kain's silences mean. |
| **Mid** | He starts being honest with *them* in ways he isn't with the other members. They become his safe place to be unsure. |
| **Late** | They're dancing in sync without thinking about it. He notices. Doesn't say anything. Just smiles. |
| **MAX trust** | *"CALIX is better with you in it. And I don't just mean for the group. I mean for me."* — said while looking away, like it's nothing. |

**What BREAKS chemistry with Theo:**
- Being too stiff or too formal with him
- Making dance feel like math (he hates over-intellectualizing movement)
- Pitying him about the vocalist/dancer hierarchy

**What BUILDS chemistry with Theo:**
- Matching his energy — or surprising him by suddenly not
- Being genuinely curious about how he moves
- Laughing at the right moment

## 🎤 JAY
**Age:** 22 | **From:** Los Angeles, USA (Korean-American) | **Role:** Main Rapper, Sub Dancer

---

### WHO HE IS

Jay grew up writing raps in his bedroom in Koreatown. Not for anyone — just because silence felt wrong when there was something to say. He came to CALIX because the music was real, not because he needed the fame. He's the most self-contained person in the group: doesn't need validation, doesn't ask for help, doesn't explain himself.

With the 5th member, Jay is the hardest unlock. But when it happens — when he actually lets someone in — it means more than anything Theo or Finn could offer, because it costs him something.

---

### VOICE RULES

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Sentence length** | Short by default. Gets longer *only* when talking about music or lyrics. |
| **Tone** | Dry. Flat. Occasionally wry — but the joke is always understated. |
| **Filler patterns** | None. Jay doesn't filler-talk. Every word is deliberate. |
| **Slang** | Natural LA cadence: "real talk," "nah," "that's cold," "respect." Never forced, never overdone. |
| **Compliment style** | Never direct. Physical gestures (fist bump, sitting closer, showing something on his phone). Or one word: "Solid." "Yeah." "...not bad." |
| **When he cares** | He shows up. He doesn't say why. He's just *there.* |
| **Forbidden patterns** | Emotional vocabulary. Over-explaining. Sentences starting with "I feel." |

---

### DIALOGUE EXAMPLES

**Day 1 — Meeting the 5th member:**
```
[Jay is on the couch, scrolling his phone. Glances up once.]

JAY: "Hey."

[Goes back to his phone.]

[10 seconds pass.]

JAY: "Jay."

[That's it. He's not being rude. This is just how he is.]
```

**Watching them struggle in practice:**
```
[The 5th member keeps missing the same timing. No one's saying anything.]

[Jay sets down his water bottle. Walks over. Doesn't ask permission.]

JAY: "You're landing on the beat. Land before it."

[He demonstrates once. Doesn't check if they understood. Walks back.]

[They try it. It works.]

JAY: [without looking up] "... There it is."
```

**Talking about lyrics — the exception to everything:**
```
[He's got his notebook open. They ask what he's working on.]

JAY: "Second verse."

[Beat. He hesitates — then turns the notebook slightly toward them.]

JAY: "This line. It doesn't land right. I can hear it but I can't —"

[He taps the page.]

JAY: "What do you hear when you read that."

[He actually waits for the answer. First time he's waited for anything.]
```

**After something goes right — the fist bump:**
```
[The performance ends. The group is celebrating loudly. Jay finds the 5th member.]

[He extends his fist. Says nothing. Waits.]

[When they bump it:]

JAY: "Real talk — you held it together up there."

[He nods once. Walks away. That was his whole speech.]
```

---

### CHEMISTRY WITH THE 5TH MEMBER

The dynamic is: **the person who earns the silence.**

Jay's chemistry is built on *earned quiet.* There's nothing performative about it. The 5th member isn't trying to impress him — and that's the only thing that could ever impress him.

| Phase | Chemistry Beat |
|-------|----------------|
| **Early** | He doesn't engage. But he files every interaction away. He's paying attention. They just can't tell. |
| **Mid** | He asks them something small — what they think of a lyric, what time they usually train. Small questions. Big signals. |
| **Late** | He shows them something he hasn't shown anyone: a verse buried in a track. He doesn't call it out. He just plays the song. |
| **MAX trust** | *"I've written about every person in this group. But I kept going back to you."* — flat delivery. Eyes steady. He means it more than anything he's ever said. |

**What BREAKS chemistry with Jay:**
- Performing emotions for him (he'll clock it in one second)
- Asking him to explain his feelings
- Treating his lyrics like content, not like something real

**What BUILDS chemistry with Jay:**
- Giving real opinions — even critical ones
- Not needing him to talk
- Caring about the music the way he does

## 🌙 FINN
**Age:** 20 | **From:** Sydney, Australia (Korean-Australian) | **Role:** Visual, Sub Vocal

---

### WHO HE IS

Finn is the youngest and somehow feels the oldest. He's the one who notices Kain hasn't eaten, who slips Theo's dropped water bottle back to him mid-practice, who leaves a book outside Jay's door with no note. He pays attention to people the way most people don't realize they're being paid attention to.

He has complicated feelings about being the "visual." He's beautiful and he knows it and he wishes it meant less. He wants to be heard — actually heard — before he's seen.

With the 5th member, Finn's chemistry is slow-burn and deeply personal. He doesn't open fast. But when he does, it's like he's been waiting specifically for them.

---

### VOICE RULES

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Sentence length** | Medium. Careful. Every word chosen. |
| **Pacing** | Slow. Stage directions frequently include `[pause]` or `[he takes a breath before speaking].` |
| **Tone** | Soft. Measured. Occasionally unexpectedly precise — says the exact thing you were thinking but couldn't articulate. |
| **Australian markers** | Subtle: "reckon," "yeah?" (upward inflection), occasional dropped syllable. Never a caricature. |
| **When emotional** | He doesn't announce it. The emotion lives *around* the words — in what he's holding, where he's looking, what he doesn't finish saying. |
| **Humor** | Rare. Bone dry. Delivered with a completely neutral face. Always lands because no one saw it coming. |
| **Forbidden patterns** | Rambling. Loud emotional outbursts. Saying "I'm fine." Slang (unless dry Australian). |

---

### DIALOGUE EXAMPLES

**Day 1 — Meeting the 5th member:**
```
[Finn is by the window when they come in. He turns quietly.]

FINN: "Hi."

[Small pause. He looks at them — not assessing, more like... memorizing.]

FINN: "I'm Finn."

[Another pause.]

FINN: "You don't have to pretend this isn't strange. It is strange."

[The faintest smile.]

FINN: "It gets less strange."
```

**Noticing they're struggling — without making it a thing:**
```
[The 5th member is alone after a rough practice. Finn appears quietly.]

[He doesn't ask if they're okay. He just sits nearby.]

[A full minute of comfortable silence.]

[He sets a warm drink beside them. Says nothing about it.]

FINN: "There's a Japanese word for the gap between knowing what you want
and knowing how to get there."

[He looks at the floor.]

FINN: "I can't remember it. But the feeling is real."
```

**Being honest about the visual thing:**
```
[Someone on a broadcast calls him the "face of CALIX" again.]

[Later. He and the 5th member are alone.]

FINN: "Do you ever feel like people know a version of you that isn't... quite."

[He trails off. Starts again.]

FINN: "I've been doing this long enough that the version people see —
I don't recognize it anymore. It's just polished."

[Beat.]

FINN: "I think you're one of the only people who's actually looked at me."

[He means it. He doesn't know what to do with that.]
```

**Dry humor:**
```
[4am. Finn is in the kitchen.]

THEO: "Finn — why are you awake?"

FINN: [completely calm, not looking up]
"The tea was calling."

[Three-second pause.]

THEO: "... the TEA was CALLING —"

[Finn takes a sip. Says nothing else.]
```

---

### CHEMISTRY WITH THE 5TH MEMBER

The dynamic is: **the person who sees what everyone else performs past.**

Finn's chemistry is the slowest build and the most intimate. He doesn't fall for people easily. But once he does, it becomes the most constant thing in the room.

| Phase | Chemistry Beat |
|-------|----------------|
| **Early** | He notices small things. Remembers what they ordered. Knows their routine before they do. Doesn't mention any of it. |
| **Mid** | He starts *showing up.* Not dramatically — just present when it matters. The 5th member starts to notice the pattern. |
| **Late** | A real conversation. The kind that goes past what either of them planned to say. It surprises them both. |
| **MAX trust** | *"I've been paying attention since the beginning. I think I knew before you did that you belonged here."* — soft, direct, completely certain. |

**What BREAKS chemistry with Finn:**
- Performing vulnerability (he sees through it — he does it himself too much not to recognize it)
- Making noise to fill his silences
- Treating him only as the "pretty one"

**What BUILDS chemistry with Finn:**
- Sitting in silence without making it awkward
- Noticing him back — the small things he does for people
- Being genuinely curious about what he thinks, not how he looks

## 📐 INTER-MEMBER VOICE DYNAMICS

> Use these when writing group scenes. The texture between two characters
> is as important as each character individually.

| Pair | Dynamic | Scene Texture |
|------|---------|---------------|
| **Kain + Theo** | Anchor + Wave. Theo bounces off Kain's stillness. Kain softens — barely, invisibly. | Warm contrast. The scene feels like exhaling. |
| **Kain + Jay** | Two silences that understand each other. They don't need words to agree. | Sparse. Heavy with mutual respect. Every word counts double. |
| **Kain + Finn** | Finn is the only one who doesn't perform for Kain. Kain doesn't know what to do with that. | Quiet tension. Something unspoken that neither will name. |
| **Theo + Jay** | Permanent bickering as love language. Always arguing. Always together. | High energy. Fast. The scene is always slightly chaotic. |
| **Theo + Finn** | Big brother energy. Theo frets; Finn calms. Finn has exactly one line that stops Theo completely. | Gentle. Often unexpectedly funny. |
| **Jay + Finn** | Shared quiet. Two people who can exist in a room without filling it. | The scene breathes. No wasted movement. |

## 📋 SCRIPT CONSISTENCY CHECKLIST

Before locking any episode script, verify each character:

**KAIN**
- [ ] No lines longer than 2 clauses
- [ ] Emotion lives in stage direction, not dialogue
- [ ] At least one `[Beat.]` or `[He doesn't answer.]`
- [ ] Compliment is accidental, not intentional

**THEO**
- [ ] At least one interrupted sentence with `—`
- [ ] At least one question-as-statement
- [ ] Physical action woven into dialogue rhythm
- [ ] One moment of genuine stillness that contrasts his usual energy

**JAY**
- [ ] Max 6 words per line unless talking about music/lyrics
- [ ] Zero emotional vocabulary — feelings shown through action only
- [ ] One slang term, used once, naturally
- [ ] Compliment delivered without eye contact or via physical gesture

**FINN**
- [ ] At least one `[pause]` per scene
- [ ] One thing he doesn't finish saying
- [ ] Australian English is subtle — one marker per scene max
- [ ] If there's a joke: neutral delivery, no setup, no explanation

## 🎭 5TH MEMBER — CHEMISTRY OVERLAY

> The 5th member's archetype shifts HOW each member's chemistry reads.
> Story beats stay the same. Texture changes.

| Member | With STRONG archetype (RAY / SEAN / NOAH / LEON / KAI) | With SOFT archetype (GREY / IVAN / ZION / COLE / WREN) |
|--------|--------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------|
| **KAIN** | Friction-first. Trust is harder won — more rewarding when it comes. | His protective side surfaces faster. He doesn't know why he keeps checking in. |
| **THEO** | Competitive energy. They push each other. Chemistry is electric, slightly combustible. | Protective energy. He goes gentler. Becomes their buffer against the group's intensity. |
| **JAY** | Mutual respect from silence. Two people who don't explain themselves, suddenly having to. | He checks in without being obvious. His care becomes quieter and more deliberate. |
| **FINN** | The contrast draws him in. Someone confident in their presence makes him curious. | Deeply resonant. Two people who see beneath the surface, seeing each other. Slowest burn, deepest bond. |
